Challenge
Design an informational type poster illustrating the unique characteristics and application of the typeface Frutiger by Adrian Frutiger. Use only keyboard characters.

Solution
After researching and discovering that Frutiger is most notably used in airports, I wanted my poster to be reminiscent of an airport gate sign. The yellow, black, and blue color scheme, layout, and arrows were all carefully chosen to create that airport feeling.
