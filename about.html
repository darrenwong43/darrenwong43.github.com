My name is Darren Wong, and I am currently studying graphic design at UC Davis. I am also a Ken Block superfan, hamburger lover, one of the few people who still watches NASCAR, excessive spender on clothes and sneakers, and aspiring Virgil Abloh.

As a kid, I loved to draw. It was just a hobby until I started watching motorsports and discovered streetwear, which introduced me to the world of graphic design. Seeing a unique race car livery or graphic tee brought me tremendous joy and made me realize that my artistic abilities could be applicable in the real world.
