Cycle News Hour is a radio show broadcasting on KDVS 90.3 FM in Davis, California. The show is written, edited, and produced by UC Davis students. Displayed above is an illustration made for a report discussing the problems with Facebook.

Challenge
Create a Cycle News Hour social media illustration for a report discussing the recent problems with Facebook.

Solution
I used a very simple illustration style complementing the modern and sleek aesthetic of tech companies. I flipped Facebook’s iconic thumbs up graphic and added negative text emojis to strongly communicate “problems with Facebook”. The entire scene depicts a moment of realization where the individual suddenly recognizes that Facebook is far from perfect.
